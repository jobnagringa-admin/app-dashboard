# Tell Me About Yourself

This is one of the most common interview questions you'll face.

## How to Structure Your Answer

1. Start with your current role
2. Briefly mention relevant experience
3. Connect to why you're interested in this opportunity

## Example Answer

"I'm currently a software engineer at XYZ Company where I lead the backend
team..."

## Tips

- Keep it under 2 minutes
- Focus on professional experience
- Tailor it to the role you're applying for
