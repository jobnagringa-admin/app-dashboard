Why do we search on these platforms?
At #jobnagringa, we highly recommend our community start by exploring opportunities with small companies and startups. We believe these companies offer the best environment for Latin American candidates for several reasons. One key reason is that these companies often promote their job openings on their websites or well-known platforms that aren't necessarily only on LinkedIn. Unfortunately, a typical search on these platforms may not easily uncover these jobs, and getting the search engine queries right can be challenging. That's why we created this tool—to simplify your search for your first opportunity. We wish you the best of luck!
